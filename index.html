Madrid is Spain's capital city, it is a city of elegant boulevards and expensive, manicured parks. It is also known as the city of the "Bear and the Strawberry Tree.




When to go and how to get there

The best time to go there is in either fall (September to November) or spring (March to May) preferibly to the weather that you prefer most but also make sure you go at a time where there isn't big crowds.

The fastest to get there is obviously by plane. But if your close enough you can go via train or car depending on the idstance but the costs shoudn't be expensive.
